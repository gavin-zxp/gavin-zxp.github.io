Welcome to Yantai Ston Metal Co., Ltd.

 About Us

Bearing the dream of being a professional supplier of steel grating, handrails, ladders and related light steel structure in serving industry engineering projects, three colleagues founded Yantai Ston Metal Co., Ltd. in the city of Yantai, China in 2019.

From the beginning, we regard ''people'' as the top important asset of the company. Therefore, we are dedicated to projects which the team can learn and improve continuously.

Thanks to the efforts of our team, as a new player in the market, we have successfully fulfilled the supply in several projects both in China and Australia. Through these records, the company is getting more and more recognition from our customers.

 Our Value

When a new company starts her business, it is not easy to promise absolute integrity, honesty and professionalism in every job. We just believe these moralities are essential for long term running of the business.

It is easy to say that reputation is important than profit, it is just hard to keep and perform. We are convinced that good reputation will definitely bring payback in due time.
